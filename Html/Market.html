Market

A diversified selection of ingredients

Italy Has Never Been Close

Take a trip through our market where you’ll find regional ingredients and an amazing variety of both Italian and local products perfect for you to take home. So get inspired and light up your pantry with a pinch of Italian excellence.
  • Il Caseificio
    Fruit and vegetables
    Watch our experts transform local milk sourced from Devon into light, flavourful cheese from fresh mozzarella to stracciatella right before your eyes. Learn about the age-old process at the mozzarella lab, enjoy the same fresh cheese in our restaurants and grab some to take home with you - the full Eataly experience.

  • I Salumi e I Formaggi
    The best Italian cured meats & cheeses
    From Prosciutto San Daniele DOP to Parmigiano Reggiano DOP, we import the finest cured meats and cheeses, offering the largest selection in the city! You can also find some of the finest British meats and cheeses from local partners such as Neal’s Yard Dairy.
  • La Macelleria
    Ethically farmed and sustainably sourced meat
    It’s our belief that the health of both the animals and the land on which they’re raised have a direct impact on the quality and flavour of the meat. We offer the best selection of La Granda, an association of breeders from Italy, promoting a native breed marked with the Slow Food presidium.
  • La Pescheria
    The freshest catch from our fishmonger
    Our seafood is always fresh and sustainably sourced, and we care about where our fish comes from and the people who catch it, both near and far. Our local and Mediterranean partners ensure the long-term vitality is considered with every catch, which means providing the best seafood for you, our fisherman and the world.
  • Pasta Fresca Bar
    Fresh pasta made in-house every day
    You’ll be able to enjoy a fresh pasta ‘show’ while our pastai, or pasta makers, transform simple ingredients into intricate pasta shapes before your eyes. Our experts are ready to share advice for cooking and pairing each freshly-shaped pasta, just ask!
