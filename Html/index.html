About us

The first Eataly opened in January 2007 in Torino, 11.000 mq spread over 3 floors, complete with 8 specialized restaurants, a coffee shop, a pastry shop, a gelateria, classrooms for cooking lessons and tastings, a wine bar, aging cellars for salumi and cheese, a beer degustation bar and the Carpano Museum.

  • Eat

    We believe with a few, high-quality ingredients, it’s possible to craft great tasting dishes. From our restaurants to fresh counters, at Eataly eating is simple.

  • Shop

    Offering a range of the best quality products from both Italy and the UK, all of our ingredients we cook with can be found in our market to buy, so you can recreate our dishes at home.

  • Learn

    The more you know, the more you enjoy. We want you to learn about the history and craft of our food, chefs and producers who inspire us whether it’s through a visit to our store, a class at La Scuola or a special event.

Our Manifesto

The name EATALY is a fusion of two words: EAT and ITALY. As a result, Eataly stands for ‘Eating Italian’ which encompasses the history and the food culture of Italy, the ease of preparing Italian dishes, and the many qualities Italy has to offer.

  • 0. We’re In Love With Food
    We love high-quality food and drink. We love the stories about it, the people who produce it, and the places it comes from.
  • 1. Food Unites Us All
