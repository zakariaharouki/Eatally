What We Do

Eataly is a place where you can eat, shop and learn under one roof. We have a variety of tastes and flavours at our restaurants and counters, authentic products in our market and fun tastings, classes and events where you can learn about Italian food, drink and culture.

Join Our Team

We dedicate our lives to promote an understanding of high-quality food and drink, and we’re always looking for members to join our London famiglia.

  • We Encourage a Culture of Curiosity, Passion, Fun and Honesty
    Curiosity and passion for products, services, our guests and experiences; harmony between our guests and team, amongst our own Eataly family and, of course, on the plate. We place a daily emphasis on creating fun and honest experiences with everyone who walks through our doors.
  • We Seek Service-Minded, Talented, and Innovative Individuals
    Eataly offers exciting opportunities that will challenge your abilities, expand your skills and reward your contributions in an atmosphere that encourages both personal and professional growth, all while continuing to offer our high standard of quality service.
